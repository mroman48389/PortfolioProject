About me

Mark studied mathematics and computer science at Western New England University. He learned object-oriented design in Java, his foundational programming language.

Mark's professional career began at Avenir Health where he developed desktop applications using Object Pascal and Delphi. In the later half of his career, the company moved on to websites and web applications. Mark started developing web apps in React with JavaScript and Material UI. After several years of frontend work, he moved on to the backend to develop the Integrated Health Tool engine in Python.

Mark enjoys working across stacks and continuous learning to build quality software that has a positive impact on people's lives.
